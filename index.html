About

Professional summary

I work at the intersection of analytics and security operations. I've learnt and built reporting pipelines for retail and finance teams, modeled churn and credit risk, and spent time on the monitoring side triaging alerts and correlating logs for security operations center. That combination shapes how I approach a new dataset, I want to know not just what the numbers show, but how the system producing them could fail or be misused.

Most of my work lives in SQL and Python, with Power BI or Tableau for the reporting layer. I've spent time inside SIEM tooling as well, which has made me more careful about data lineage and access than I might otherwise be. I like projects with a clear business question attached, a churn number a retention team can act on, a risk score a lender can defend, an alert threshold a SOC team can actually live with.
